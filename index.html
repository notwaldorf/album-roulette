Prepare your spreadsheet
Your CSV needs at least two columns: one for the band/artist and one for the album title. A third column for "last listened" date is optional but lets the app track what you've heard. Export from Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app as .csv.

Configure column letters below
Tell the app which column holds each field. Defaults are A, B, C. If your sheet has a header row, leave the option set to "Header row."
3
Drop or choose your file
Everything is stored locally in your browser — nothing leaves your device. Re-uploading later will add new albums while keeping any listen dates you've logged.
